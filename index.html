About Us

Who We Are & What We Believe

Kingdom Light Church

Kingdom Light Church is a Christ-centered, Spirit-led community of believers dedicated to spreading the Gospel and demonstrating God’s love through worship, service, and outreach. We are committed to raising a generation that walks in light, truth, and power.

Our Mission

To lead souls to Christ, equip believers with sound biblical teachings, and empower them to shine as light in every sphere of influence.

Our Vision

To become a global beacon of hope and transformation, where lives are changed by the power of God’s word and love.

Our Leadership

Led by Monica Brazell, our church fosters a culture of humility, excellence, and purpose through Spirit-filled leadership.

Our Services

What we offer to the body of Christ

Worship Services

Join us for powerful worship experiences that connect you to God through praise, worship, and the Word.

Prayer Meetings

Weekly intercessory sessions where we seek God's face for transformation, healing, and breakthroughs.

Bible Study

In-depth teachings and discussions to help you grow in biblical knowledge and Christian maturity.

Youth & Teens Ministry

Equipping the younger generation to live out their faith boldly in school, work, and society.

Outreach & Evangelism

Spreading the love of Christ in our community through missions, charity, and community impact programs.

Discipleship

Mentorship and training programs that help believers become true disciples of Jesus Christ.
