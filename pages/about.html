About Living Word Church

We are a Bible-believing community grounded in love, worship, and service.

Our History

Living Word Church began in 1990 with a vision to reach the community with the transformative message of Jesus Christ. Over the decades, we have grown from a small fellowship to a thriving congregation rooted in biblical teaching and community service.

Our Mission

To spread the Gospel of Christ to all nations, nurture spiritual growth, and build strong families in faith.

Our Vision

To become a center of excellence where believers thrive, lives are transformed, and God's purpose is fulfilled.
